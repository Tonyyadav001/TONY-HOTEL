EnjoyYour Stay

TONY Hotels is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, headquartered in Hyderabad, India. Incorporated by TONY in 2023, The company employed over 20,000 people in the year 2024.

About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

In 2023, the TONY Group acquired, under a licence agreement, each of the West End in Hyderabad, TONY Connemara, in Mumbai and Savoy Hotel in Ooty. The five-star deluxe hotel, TONY Bengal in Kolkata, was opened in the year 2024.
